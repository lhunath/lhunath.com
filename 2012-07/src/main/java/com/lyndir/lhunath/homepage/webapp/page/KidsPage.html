On the 13th of June, 2008, I held my beautiful little daughter. On September 19th, 2011, two awesome boys joined the ranks.

My life hasn't quite been the same since.

Only half a meter long, and barely over 2.75kg, Ophelia was alive and perfectly healthy. I'd heard stories of these things, but actually standing there and holding such a fragile, perplexed, confused and amazed little thing — no sentence I write here could convey the experience.

Three years later and I was back there, in that room. Same situation, except for the small detail that the fork had forked itself this time. George and Theodore were identical twins. Not quite the same, though: Theodore, the first one out, was no heavier than 1.56kg. George was quite a bit heavier at 2.28kg. Theodore stayed in the NICU for over a month, George got out after two weeks.
